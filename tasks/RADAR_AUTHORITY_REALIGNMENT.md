## Business closure

**Given:** the active authority routes development into a long bounded Fixed-Policy evidence run
before production Radar input reachability has been established.

**When:** the authority chain is realigned around one continuously consumed public fact stream,
changed-state-driven incremental structure assessment, localized availability, structure-level
denominators, forward risk scenarios that do not imply holding time, and asynchronous Outcome
maturity.

**Then:** later AI agents are authorized to establish production Radar reachability first and
cannot treat an all-`UNKNOWN` bounded run, replay equality, repeated computation over static
facts, a risk-scenario count, or evidence-bundle completion as a usable Fixed-Policy business
baseline.

**Independent verification:** a fresh authority read and repository-wide consistency search find
one next closure, one streaming runtime lifecycle, no active authority that makes a one-hour
warm-up or six-hour evidence window the product processing unit, no unchanged-state evaluation loop,
and no active text that turns forward risk scenarios into holding periods or opportunity
multipliers.

**Valid zero/UNKNOWN result:** existing all-`UNKNOWN`, zero-assessment evidence remains honest
diagnostic evidence. It does not close `STRUCTURE_ASSESSMENT_REACHABILITY` or prove zero Policy
opportunity.

**Upstream prerequisite:** explicit human authorization to amend the authority chain, supplied by
the request to correct the documented product direction.

## Product operating behavior

The long-term product uses one shared public fact stream. Each accepted fact updates rolling state;
only a changed consumed economic state or necessary time boundary triggers assessment of the
current authorized universe. Implementation may recompute that universe or optimize affected
structures. An unchanged state may update collector health but creates no assessment, Decision,
Candidate observation, opportunity, or business artifact. The current prospective permission
stops after continuous acquisition and structure assessment; it does not authorize a target Policy
action or long-running admission/Outcome cohort.

## Acceptance

### Direct behavior

1. Authority says acquisition, state update, and structure assessment are one streaming flow; a
   changed consumed fact or necessary time boundary triggers evaluation, and bounded evidence
   windows do not define Online Runtime lifetime. Implementation may recompute the current universe
   or optimize affected structures without requiring a generic dependency index.
2. Authority says duplicate, heartbeat, unrelated, replayed, and arbitrary timer triggers over an
   unchanged `evaluation_state_digest` create no assessment, Decision, opportunity, or Radar
   business artifact. Raw wall time, continuous age/TTE movement, and `capture_seq` alone are not
   necessary time boundaries.
3. Authority separates global readiness, universe coverage, per-structure readiness, structure
   assessment, future Policy action, admission, and Outcome maturity. Local availability failures
   affect dependent structures; a changed declared global aggregate affects every named consumer
   without being mislabeled as local.
4. `CURRENT_STAGE` says production Radar reachability is not established and authorizes only
   `STRUCTURE_ASSESSMENT_REACHABILITY` next.
5. Structure-assessment acceptance requires two distinct `evaluation_state_digest` values under
   one continuous collector, direct same-state suppression tests, and at least one state with
   nonzero legal, round-trip-executable, and risk-assessable structure counts, at least one
   calculated risk-scenario slot, and no all-not-applicable structure counted as risk-assessable.
   It does not require a Policy action.
6. Authority says one unique legal structure is one assessment unit and its four forward risk
   scenarios are diagnostic components, not holding periods or four opportunities; each scenario
   slot is `CALCULATED | NOT_APPLICABLE_TTE | UNKNOWN`.
7. Before any Candidate action, a later closure must separately authorize structure-level scenario
   aggregation, an Entry Policy, a state-driven position-management Policy, hard exit boundaries,
   and a new forward Outcome identity. Historical `horizon_seconds` exit behavior is not inherited.
8. The structure-assessment-reachability closure does not require Candidate, Entry, mature Outcome,
   a six-hour run, RunReceipt, historical archive, multi-layer drift bundle, or Policy tuning.
9. Evidence and replay requirements are `REQUIRED` or `NOT_APPLICABLE` according to the task
   assertion; structure-assessment reachability reuses existing inspection support and creates no
   Radar receipt/witness identity, replay artifact, or drift taxonomy.
10. The absent production scheduled-block source is not replaced. The target assessment does not
    consume it; no document fabricates `CLEAR` or silently decides the later Entry/admission rule.
